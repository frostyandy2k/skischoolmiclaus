Your Instructors

The Miclaus brothers love skiing and they wish to gift this passion to others. They are officially recognized ski instructors (monitor schi) having followed a romanian-austrian schi instructor training in 2019.

Big ski enthusiast, Andrei and Alex have been skiing since they were 4. They spent their first seasons of skiing in the mountains of baisoara, an hours drive from the beautiful city of Cluj-Napoca.
